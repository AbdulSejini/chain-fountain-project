What is it?

The Chain Fountain

Imagine dropping a chain of beads from a high place. Instead of just falling down, the chain leaps UP into the air like a magical fountain before falling! 🤯

1️⃣
Stack the Chain
Put a long bead chain neatly in a container, like coiling a snake 🐍
2️⃣
Let it Drop!
Pull one end over the edge and gravity starts pulling the chain down ⬇️
3️⃣
Watch the Magic!
The chain rises UP above the container like a fountain before falling! ⛲✨
This is called The Mould Effect
Named after Steve Mould who made it famous on YouTube! 📺
⚡
The Science

How Does It Work?

It's all about Newton's 3rd Law: Every action has an equal and opposite reaction! When the chain pushes DOWN on the container, the container pushes it back UP! 🚀

⚖️
Newton's 3rd Law
ACTION = REACTION
Push down ⬇️ → Bounce up ⬆️

Kick Effect

⚖️
Kinetic Energy
Tap me!
Energy from falling height turns into upward motion — higher drop = more energy! ⬆️

Fun Facts

Did You Know?

📺 Who discovered this effect? ▼
Steve Mould made a YouTube video that went viral with millions of views! Even top university physicists were surprised! 🤯
📏 Does chain length matter? ▼
A longer chain makes the fountain last longer ⏱️ but doesn't make it jump higher. Only the drop height changes the fountain's height!

We want to test heights above 200cm, try plastic vs metal chains, different containers, and film with ultra slow-motion cameras! 🎬🚀
